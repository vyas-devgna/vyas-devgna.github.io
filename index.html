ThrillYaari

Unified Travel Operations & Management Platform

A comprehensive digital operating system designed to replace spreadsheets and WhatsApp with a centralized, accountable, and financial-first management tool for end-to-end trip execution.

The Business Problem

Running a travel company involves dozens of moving parts. Currently, operations are fragmented and risky:

  • Information Silos: Trip details live in Excel, driver details in WhatsApp, expenses in pockets.
  • Financial Leakage: Cash given to drivers/guides is hard to track. Over-budgeting is noticed only after the trip ends.
  • Vendor Chaos: "Who is the driver for the Manali group?" leads to frantic phone calls.
  • Lack of Real-time Visibility: Owners don't know if a guest has checked in or if a vehicle has broken down until it's a crisis.

The Solution: One Source of Truth

ThrillYaari Platform connects the Head Office (Planning & Finance) with the Field Staff (Guides & Drivers) in real-time.

  • Centralized Booking & Planning: Every trip is a digital project.
  • Real-time Field App: Guides and Vendors see their schedule and report statuses via mobile.
  • Live Expense Tracking: Cash flows are recorded immediately.
  • Vendor CRM: A permanent database of hotels, drivers, and activity providers.

User Roles & Hierarchy

Admin / Owner
Full visibility. Financial approvals. Vendor settlements. Profit/Loss analysis.
Ops Manager
Trip creation. Resource assignment (Guide/Car). Monitoring active trips.
Travel Agent (Sales)
Input leads. Convert to bookings. View availability.
Trip Leader / Guide
Mobile access. View itinerary. Upload bills. Report incidents. Mark attendance.
Vendor (Transport)
Receive booking orders. Assign driver/vehicle details. Upload invoices.
Vendor (Hotel)
Receive rooming lists. Confirm check-ins. Submit final bills.

Financial Control Mechanism

The system enforces a strict "Maker-Checker" financial discipline to prevent leakage.

1. Allocation

Before the trip, the Admin allocates a Trip Wallet (e.g., ₹20,000) to the Guide. This is recorded as a liability against the Guide.

2. Utilization

During the trip, the Guide logs expenses via the App. They must upload a photo of the bill. The wallet balance decreases in real-time.

3. Settlement

After the trip, the Guide cannot be assigned a new trip until they Settle the balance (return unspent cash or get reimbursed).

Why This System is Crucial

  • Scalability: You cannot run 50 trips a month using WhatsApp groups. This system allows you to handle 10x volume with the same staff.
  • Valuation: A business with digital systems and clean data is worth significantly more to investors than a chaotic agency.
  • Vendor Trust: Vendors prefer working with agencies that have transparent payment ledgers and automated booking orders.
  • Owner Freedom: The business owner can travel or take a break without operations collapsing, as the system enforces the process.
